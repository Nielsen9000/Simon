How We Started

Frustrated by everything that made sensitive skin worse.

BarrierLab started with a simple frustration: too many "sensitive skin" products still contain fragrance, alcohol, and unnecessary fillers. Products that look premium on the shelf but routinely trigger flares, redness, and breakouts.

The problem wasn't a lack of options — it was a lack of honesty. Too many brands put fragrance in a moisturiser for the sensory experience and call it "calming." Too many serums contain irritating actives and get labelled "gentle."

So we stripped everything back and built a small, focused range around one principle: barrier health first. Every ingredient earns its place. Every product is formulated to protect and repair — not just to perform on a shelf.

The Science

Why the Skin Barrier is Everything

The skin barrier — the stratum corneum — is your skin's protective outer layer. When it's healthy, it keeps moisture in and irritants out. When it's damaged, your skin becomes reactive, dry, inflamed, and sensitive to almost everything.

Most skincare inadvertently damages the barrier: harsh cleansers strip lipids, alcohol-based toners disrupt the acid mantle, and fragrance triggers immune responses in the skin.

Every product is formulated around barrier-supporting ingredients — humectants, gentle actives, and soothing botanicals that repair without irritating.
